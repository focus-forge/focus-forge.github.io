2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

App Settings and Preferences

When you use FocusForge, you provide:

  • Focus session settings (duration, break times, goals)
  • App blocking preferences (which apps to block)
  • Personal notes and reflections
  • Daily routine configurations
  • Task information (titles, priorities, categories)
  • Notification preferences

Important: This information is stored locally on your device and never sent to our servers.

2.2 Health and Fitness Data (HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, we access the following data from Apple HealthKit:

What We Access:

  • Step count — to track your daily activity and movement
  • Active energy burned — to measure exercise intensity
  • Workout sessions — to understand your exercise routine and integrate with your focus sessions

How We Use Your HealthKit Data:

  • Display your fitness progress in the FocusForge app
  • Provide personalized insights and recommendations
  • Track your activity goals alongside your focus goals
  • Motivate you during focus sessions
  • Show workout completion in your daily progress

Your HealthKit Data:

  • ✓ Is stored locally on your device only
  • ✓ Never leaves your device or is sent to our servers
  • ✓ Is never shared with third parties
  • ✓ Is not used for advertising or marketing
  • ✓ Is not sold or disclosed to data brokers
  • ✓ Remains under your complete control

What We Do NOT Do With HealthKit Data:

  • ✗ Use health data for advertising or marketing
  • ✗ Sell health data to anyone
  • ✗ Share health data with data brokers
  • ✗ Use health data for purposes unrelated to app functionality
  • ✗ Track you across other apps using health data

2.3 Screen Time and App Usage Permissions

We use Apple's Screen Time API (FamilyControls framework) to enable app blocking during your focus sessions.

What We Can Do:

  • Block or shield apps you select
  • Set time limits on app usage during focus sessions
  • Restrict app access during scheduled routines
  • Apply Loose Block (with limited bypasses)
  • Apply Strict Block (Pro feature - no bypasses)

What We Cannot Do:

  • See which apps you use
  • Track how much time you spend in apps
  • Access your device usage history
  • Monitor your browsing or app activity
  • View your Screen Time statistics

Privacy Protections:

Apple's Screen Time API is designed with privacy-first principles:

  • All app blocking settings are stored locally on your device
  • We do not collect or have access to information about which apps you use or when you use them
  • Your device activity data remains completely private
  • We can only apply blocking restrictions — we cannot see usage patterns

2.4 Subscription and Purchase Data

When you subscribe to FocusForge Pro through the App Store, Apple shares limited information with us through our subscription management service (Superwall).

What We Receive from Apple:

  • Anonymous Subscriber ID (unique to you and our app, NOT your Apple ID)
  • Subscription status (active, expired, or cancelled)
  • Subscription tier (annual plan at $79/year)
  • Purchase date and renewal date
  • Your country or region (for tax and currency purposes)
  • Device type (iPhone or iPad) and iOS version

What We Do NOT Receive:

  • Your name, email address, or Apple ID
  • Payment card information or billing address
  • Any personally identifiable payment information
  • Your purchase history from other apps

  • ✗ Selling to third parties or data brokers
  • ✗ Tracking across other apps or websites
  • ✗ Building user profiles for unrelated purposes
  • ✗ Behavioral targeting or profiling

5. Sharing Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information to anyone.

We share limited information only in these specific circumstances:

5.1 With Apple

  • Subscription validation through App Store
  • iCloud backup (only if you enable iCloud sync)
  • HealthKit integration (data remains local, Apple facilitates access)
  • Screen Time API (Apple provides framework, no data shared)

5.2 With Superwall (Subscription Management)

Data Shared:

  • Purchase events and subscription status
  • Paywall interaction data (views, dismissals, conversions)
  • Anonymous user identifiers
  • Device information (model, OS, region)

Contractual Protection: Superwall is contractually prohibited from using your data for their own purposes, selling your data to third parties, or using your data for advertising.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your personal information. Here's how to exercise your rights:

6.1 Access Your Data

Email us at privacy@focusforge.com to request a copy of your data. We will provide it within 30 days in a readable format (PDF or JSON).

6.2 Delete Your Data

You have three options to delete your data:

  1. Delete the App - Removes all local data immediately
  2. In-App Deletion - Settings → Privacy → Delete All Data
  3. Request Deletion - Email privacy@focusforge.com

6.3 Revoke Permissions

HealthKit Access:

  1. iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health
  2. Select FocusForge
  3. Turn off any or all data types

Screen Time Access:

  1. iOS Settings → Screen Time
  2. Select FocusForge
  3. Tap Remove

6.4 For European Union Users (GDPR Rights)

  • Right to Access

  • Right to Restrict Processing

  • Right to Object

6.5 For California Users (CCPA/CPRA Rights)

If you're a California resident, you have these rights under CCPA/CPRA:
